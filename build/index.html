FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find an answer? Contact our team.

Can NeverSEO replace an SEO team?

No. NeverSEO is a powerful tool that helps SEO teams work more efficiently, faster, and with fewer errors. The system automates analysis and operations, but strategic thinking is ultimately driven by humans.

I'm new to SEO. Can I use it?

Absolutely. NeverSEO is designed with a user-friendly interface. The system guides you step-by-step, like having an SEO expert by your side.

Will Google penalize AI-written articles?

Google does not penalize AI-generated content; they penalize spam and low-quality content. NeverSEO uses AI as an assistant to help you research and write better, providing genuinely useful information for readers.

Is my company's data secure?

Yes. Each Organization has a completely independent data space. Sensitive information is highly encrypted and never shared with any third party.

Can I use my own AI accounts?

Yes. On the Enterprise plan, you can connect your AI accounts (OpenAI, Google API Keys) to manage token costs yourself and have more control at scale.

Does NeverSEO guarantee keyword rankings?

Any ranking guarantee is unrealistic because Google's algorithm constantly changes. NeverSEO helps you build a solid SEO foundation and create quality content to grow your customer base sustainably over the long term.

Can I cancel or change plans anytime?

Yes. You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time right from your account settings, with no long-term contracts.
